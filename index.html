About Us

If you know something good then why not share it with others, afterall happiness is all which matters and it increases by sharing. Spreading happiness is all our motto. This is how it all started: To impress a girl one of our friend (a good cook) made some chocolates and sent it to his girl. To make a good impression on her, he asked her what all different kind of chocolates you like (like he knows everything) and as you know about girls, she named 5-6 different kind of chocolates. Then his struggle started and he researched about different kind of chocolates and how they can be made at home. Finally, he gifted her all kind of chocolates which she said and it was a happy ending :). After looking at his success our another friend followed the same path and it was also a happy ending. We then decided we can share this happiness with others and have more success stories. So, we started making chocolates at our home and started sharing it. Everyone gave good feedback on our chocolates and we finally decided to reach out to everyone and then we started spreading happiness.

These are our guiding principles :

  • The quality of product should not be compromised in any case.
  • Taste should be like, if someone eats one should remember us always.
  • We are excited about building strong relationships with everyone we interact with: our customers, our community, and our suppliers.
  • We want to improve everyday and be the best in taste.
